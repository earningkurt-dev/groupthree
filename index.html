Program Overview

The Bachelor of Industrial Technology major in Computer and Information Technology is designed to develop technically skilled graduates who are competent in computer systems, networking, programming, industrial automation, and IT support services. The program blends hands-on laboratory training with theoretical foundations to equip students for industry-ready careers.

About DOIT

Davao Oriental International Technology College (DOIT) is a premier institution dedicated to providing high-quality, industry-aligned education in Davao Oriental. Guided by a commitment to academic excellence and innovation, DOIT prepares students to succeed in an increasingly competitive and technology-driven world.

As an institution focused on lifelong learning and global competence, DOIT offers a diverse selection of programs in technology, business, education, and other emerging fields. Each program is designed to cultivate practical skills, critical thinking, and professional integrity—ensuring that graduates are well-equipped for local and international opportunities.

DOIT’s faculty is composed of qualified and experienced educators who are passionate about teaching and student development. Supported by modern facilities and a conducive learning environment, the college provides a strong foundation for academic growth, hands-on training, and holistic formation.

Beyond academics, DOIT is committed to fostering community engagement, cultural appreciation, and leadership. Through partnerships with industries, organizations, and local communities, the institution continuously enhances its programs to meet global standards and contribute to regional development.

With its dedication to innovation, service, and excellence, Davao Oriental International Technology College stands as a trusted partner in shaping future-ready professionals and empowering the next generation of leaders.
